LCDI at Enfuse 2017 from Dillon Deloge on Vimeo.

Every year, the LCDI sends a select group of students to Guidance Software's annual Enfuse Conference. This year, we asked students to reflect on their experiences. I filmed and edited all the interviews for this project. Our students filmed all the b-roll on GoPros.

LCDI 2016-17 Wrap Up Video from Dillon Deloge on Vimeo.

This is a social media video for the Leahy Center for Digital Investigation (LCDI). The goal was to recap to our followers the awesome year we've had at the LCDI and thank all the people who engage with us.

Footage at the beginning is from High School Musical 2. All other footage was captured by me in the LCDI. All animation was done by me and the graphic at the end was made by our graphic designer.

The Grand Master Development Reel from Dillon Deloge on Vimeo.

This is a reel for a group of Champlain College students who made a video game for their Capstone project. It's called The Grand Master. It is a resource management game with elements of action and adventure.

I did all the filming and interviewing you see and did the little animation at the beginning and end. The team's lead artist drew their logo.
